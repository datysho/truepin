# Supporters

TruePin is free and complete - the contribution changes nothing in the extension. This list is the thank-you: people who chipped in through the voluntary Support button (extension Options - Support).

To get listed after contributing, open an issue titled "Add me to SUPPORTERS" with the name (and optional link) you want shown. Entries are added manually after matching against the payment records, so a real contribution is behind every name here.
