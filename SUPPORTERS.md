# Supporters 💛

TruePin is free forever, with no ads and no tracking - and the people here
chose to support it anyway. Every line is a real person who bought this
project a little more time.

Donated and want your line? **[Open a claim issue](https://github.com/datysho/truepin/issues/new?title=I%20donated%20%E2%98%95&body=Name%2C%20handle%20or%20link%20for%20SUPPORTERS.md%3A%0A%0A%28or%20say%20%22anonymous%22%20to%20be%20counted%2C%20not%20named%29)**
or reply to your PayPal receipt with the name, handle or link you want
listed - or say "anonymous" and the counter below grows instead.
